Welcome to Chef's Choice.

This is a web application where the chef can decide for you what you are going to eat!

Sometimes it is hard to pick something to cook right? No worries, the Chef is here for you!

Instructions

  • Use the "Ingredient" tab to input the ingredients you usually use.
  • Use the "Add Recipe" tab to create the recipes you like. Servings should be for person.
  • You can always view the recipes that you have in your portfolio in the "Recipes" tab. You can also remove recipes that you don't want.
  • Finally, use the "Meal Decider" tab for the Chef to choose a meal from your portfolio so that you don't have the fuss of choosing what to cook!
  • Further, if you'd like to have a whole week planned by the Chef in the "Weekly Planner" tab. It will also provide you a grocery list for the week according to the meals per person!
  • The "Weekly Plan" tab saves your weekly plan for you to check it out!
